Our Story

Founded in 2010 in Shenzhen, China, Yukoli began with a simple mission: to revolutionize commercial kitchens through intelligent automation. What started as a small team of engineers has grown into a global leader in smart kitchen solutions.

Today, our equipment powers kitchens across 45+ countries, from bustling fast-food chains in Southeast Asia to high-end restaurants in Europe. We've helped thousands of businesses reduce labor costs, improve consistency, and scale their operations efficiently.
